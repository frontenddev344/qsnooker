Refunds and Returns Policy

Refunds and Returns Policy

At Q-Snooker , we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Membership Fees

  • Non-Refundable: Membership fees are non-refundable once payment has been processed. This policy is in place to ensure that we can maintain the quality and availability of our facilities for all members.

  • Cancellation: If you choose to cancel your membership, you will retain access to club facilities until the end of your current membership term. No refunds will be issued for any unused portion of your membership.

Tournament Fees

  • Event Cancellations: Tournament entry fees are generally non-refundable. However, if you need to withdraw from a tournament due to unforeseen circumstances, please notify us at least [insert timeframe, e.g., 48 hours] before the event. Refunds may be issued at our discretion based on the circumstances.

  • Event Cancellations: In the event that a tournament is canceled by Q-Snooker, all entry fees will be fully refunded to participants.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for a tournament entry fee, please contact us at info@q-snooker.com with your details, including:

  • Name

  • Membership or tournament registration number

  • Reason for refund request

Feedback and Support

We value your feedback and are here to assist you. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Refunds and Returns Policy, please reach out to us. Your satisfaction is important to us, and we aim to provide the best possible experience at Q-Snooker.
