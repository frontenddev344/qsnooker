Seniors Open

  • Format: Knockout format with a maximum of 64 players. Matches are best of 5 frames until the semi-finals, which are best of 7 frames. The final is best of 9 frames.

  • Eligibility: Open to players aged 50 and over.

  • Entry Fee: $50

  • Details: A prestigious event celebrating the skill and experience of our senior players. The tournament will be held over three days with a grand final on the last day.

Mid Amateur Championships

  • Format: : Round-robin group stage followed by knockout rounds. Groups of 4 players, with the top 2 from each group advancing to the knockout stage.

  • Eligibility: Open to players with a handicap rating between 10 and 30.

  • Entry Fee: $40

  • Details: Aimed at mid-level amateurs looking to test their skills in a competitive yet friendly environment. Matches are played over a best of 3 frames format.

  • Format: Team-based league format with matches played in a round-robin structure. Each team consists of 3 players.

  • Details: A league competition designed to nurture young talent and provide them with valuable match experience. Teams will compete in monthly fixtures throughout the season.
